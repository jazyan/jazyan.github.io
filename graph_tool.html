current toggled edge color

  • double-click to create a node
  • click to select or deselect a node or edge
  • click a node, then right-click another to create an edge between them
  • hold click on a node to drag it and its associated edges around
  • hold click anywhere else in canvas to drag entire graph
  • shift to color a selected edge blue or red
  • backspace to delete selected node or edge
  • tab to toggle color of subsequently created edges
  • ctrl to toggle whether the edges are directed or undirected
